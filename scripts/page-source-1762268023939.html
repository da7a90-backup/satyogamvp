Namaste & Welcome,
The core purpose of the Sat Yoga Ashram is to facilitate a deep process of inner transformation for visitors and residents alike. Participating in one of our programs can be a powerful and life-changing event augmented by extended immersion in an energy field dedicated to spiritual growth and self-transcendence.
 
To protect the integrity of this spiritual refuge and "transformatorium," we want to ensure that all who come to stay with us have a complete understanding of our path and vision, feel naturally aligned with what we offer, and are ready to embark on this adventure.
 
While the Sat Yoga Ashram is a space of deep peace, play, creativity, and rejuvenating natural beauty, the time spent at our ashram requires courage and a spirit of joyful participation.
 
In this application, you will have the opportunity to introduce yourself, tell us about your background, and describe your motivation and readiness for the program you are applying for. You will also be asked to share information about your professional skills and about areas that you could contribute to.
 
Once we have reviewed your application form, we may arrange a phone interview with you or request more information via email in order to consider your application in more detail.
 
We look forward to hearing from you soon.
 
Namaste,
Sat Yoga Ashram
How does it work?
Our Sevadhari Program is a three- to six-month immersion in yogic life for those who are ready for a serious and direct spiritual path to liberation. In exchange for around 25 hours per week of work, sevadharis will live at our ashram and participate in all our community classes as well as the Sat Yoga approach to transformation (including study, inner work, meditation, and seva).
Who can apply?
We welcome an application from anyone who is willing to dive into our way of life and our accelerated mode of healing. We are looking for those who can work stably and harmoniously within the flow of our ashram needs and schedule. We are especially interested in those applicants who already listen to Shunyamurti’s talks and resonate with them, and who would like to gain deeper understanding of the Sat Yoga view of reality. This program is for those who connect with our approach and our community dharma. To read more about our approach, click here.
